About Our College

SODIQ ADELEKE COLLEGE OF ARABIC AND ISLAMIC STUDIES is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education in Arabic language and Islamic studies. We build a supportive environment where students grow in knowledge, character, and confidence.

Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society.

Our History

Founded with the vision of becoming a center of excellence for Arabic language and Islamic studies, the college has expanded its programs and student support services to serve learners from diverse backgrounds.

Today, we remain committed to academic excellence, character development, and a vibrant campus community.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence: Strong teaching, relevant curriculum, and measurable growth.
  • Inclusivity: A welcoming community for every learner.
  • Integrity: Honest practice in all academic and administrative matters.
  • Innovation: Creative learning approaches and student support.
