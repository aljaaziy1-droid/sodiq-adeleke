Admission Requirements

Welcome to SODIQ ADELEKE COLLEGE OF ARABIC AND ISLAMIC STUDIES! We are excited to inform you about our admission process and requirements.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission to our college, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a high school graduate or equivalent.
  • Have a minimum GPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Submit all required documentation before the deadline.

Application Process

The application process for admission to our college is straightforward:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit the required documents, including transcripts and test scores.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend an interview if shortlisted.

Deadlines

Please note the following important deadlines:

  • Fall semester applications due: March 1st
  • Spring semester applications due: October 1st

We encourage all prospective students to apply early to ensure consideration for admission. If you have any questions about the application process or requirements, please do not hesitate to contact our admissions office.

We look forward to welcoming you to SODIQ ADELEKE COLLEGE OF ARABIC AND ISLAMIC STUDIES and supporting you on your academic journey!
